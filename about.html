About Spoken Hindi Class

Helping Tamil speakers learn Hindi with confidence, clarity, and culture.

Our Mission

SpokenHindi was created with one simple goal: to make learning Hindi natural and stress-free for Tamil speakers. Instead of generic courses, we built a platform where every sentence, every letter, and every grammar rule is explained bilingually — in Tamil and English — so nothing gets lost in translation.

We believe that real fluency comes from practice, not memorization. That's why our core feature is interactive: you translate real Tamil sentences into Hindi, and get immediate, detailed feedback on grammar and spelling — just like a personal tutor.
