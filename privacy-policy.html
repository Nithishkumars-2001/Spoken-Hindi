Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your information.

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Welcome to SpokenHindi ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle any information collected through our website. This Privacy Policy explains our practices in simple terms.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Form Data: When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message content.
  • Practice Activity: Answers you type into practice modules are processed locally in your browser to provide feedback and are not transmitted to any external server unless you explicitly submit them via a form.
  • Usage Data: Basic, non-identifying information such as browser type and pages visited may be collected to improve the website experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • To improve our learning content, practice exercises, and website functionality.
  • To understand which features are most useful to learners, in aggregate and anonymized form.

Data Protection

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Any data you provide is used solely for the purposes described in this policy and is stored securely.

Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies or local browser storage to remember your practice progress and preferences. These do not track you across other websites.

Your Rights

  • You may request access to any personal data we hold about you.
  • You may request correction or deletion of your data at any time.
  • You may opt out of any future communications from us.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed to be family-friendly and safe for learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental guidance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page or email us at support@spokenhindi.com.
